NOISE CANCELLING GO

Noise Cancelling go is the perfect tool that you need in the classroom in order to keep your class quiet , silent and entertained while enjoying the process and being not only entertained by it but also rewarded.

What is it?

An app that you can use in the classroom in order to better help you keep a quiet enviroment. Familiar Pokemon faces will come out to help you delight students and surprise them.

How to?

Just keep the app open. It automatically detects the sound level in the room. The longer the room stays quiet the more Pokemon will come out and the more rare they will get. If the level of noise increases the Pokemons will go away and hide.

Who is it for?

For teachers and students alike. Teachers have the benefit of using Pokemons to help them keep the room quiet. Students can also sign up as students and collect Pokemons from teachers the more quiet they stay in class.
